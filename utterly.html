Project:
Utterly

Learn a language. Find your voice.

Utterly.

Utterly is the design for a web app that enables language teachers to advertise their services and independent language learners to discover and book them.

The design is available as a native app for both iOS and Android.

 

My roles

End-to-end product design
Native iOS app design
Native Android app design
User testing
User interviews

Duration

April-May 2021

The users

I developed composite user profiles based on interviews and active social listening.

Styles

Style guides for the native iOS and Android apps were designed in accordance with the native components, aesthetics, and standards described in the 2021 iOS Human-Interface Guidelines and Material Design documentation, respectively.

iOS palette

The iOS palette includes its native grayscale shades.

 

Android palette

The Android palette follows the Material Design approach to using multiple shades and tints of primary and secondary colors.

 

Typography

The iOS version of the design utilizes variations of iOS' native San Francisco typeface.

The Android version of the design utilizes variations of Roboto, in accordance with Material Design guidelines.

 

Sample iOS UI components

Native components and UI patterns are used whenever available.

 

Sample Android UI components

Native components and UI patterns are used whenever available.

Final design

Utterly was designed to support independent language teachers' as they professionalize and language learners' passion for learning on a single easy, flexible, secure platform.
