Project:
Sky Parlor

Web and native app design for North Carolina artists and artistry

Sky Parlor is an e-commerce web app featuring the work of traditional and contemporary artists, artisans, and makers living and working in North Carolina, United States.

The problem

North Carolina is already a destination for the artistically inclined and home to a number of thriving art communities, retreat centers, and schools. Sky Parlor was designed to help support NC artists by increasing the visibility and sales of their work and attracting further arts-related tourism to the state.

The objective

To provide North Carolina artists and artisans with a marketplace to showcase and sell their work and to promote North Carolina tourism through increasing the visibility of its artistry.

Logo

The Sky Parlor brand logo needed to strike a balance between minimalism and folksiness. In my first iteration of the design, I combined a classic typeface with a simple circle in a modern gradient suggesting the sun setting over the North Carolina mountains.

I then refined the logo to reinforce the North Carolina branding more emphatically by turning the "sun" into an outline of the state of North Carolina with the same sunset gradient. Preference testing indicated that most users found this version both easier to understand and more visually appealing.
